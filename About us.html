About Us:

A and M Restaurant is your number one restaurant for all your healthy meals, Juices and smoothies. With a focus on customer service and uniqueness we are here to satisfy our customer's needs. Founded in 2018 by Authalee Barrett and Murray Benneth.
A and M restaurant has come a long way from its beginnings in a cook shop, in Kingston 20. When they first started out, their passion for providing healthy meals drove them to doing intense research to improve on the quality of the meals provided and what would best suite the customers . They quit their day jobs, and gave their impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to spectaclar restaurant.
They now serve customers all over Kingston and will be opening a new location in Manchester in 2019.
We hope you enjoy our meals as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us page for more information.
